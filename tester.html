Our Story

Founded in 1995, Golden Dragon has been serving authentic Chinese cuisine for over 25 years. Our chef, Master Li, brings decades of experience from the Sichuan province of China.

We use only the freshest ingredients and traditional cooking techniques to bring you the most authentic flavors. Many of our recipes have been passed down through generations.
